6H2F | X-ray crystallography

Aeromonas heamolytic toxin B pre-pore structure

Part of the tripartite pore forming protein family, AhlB interacts with AhlA and AhlC to form a membrane spanning pore. We determined the structure of AhlB in both the soluble and pre-pore state, as well as the AhlC soluble state, which gave us insight into how these proteins function.
